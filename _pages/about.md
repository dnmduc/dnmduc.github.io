I received the B.Eng. and M.Eng. degrees in Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering from [Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology, Vietnam](https://hcmut.edu.vn/) in 2005 and 2007, respectively, and the Ph.D. degree in Computer Engineering from [Kyung Hee University, Korea](https://khu.ac.kr) in 2014. My research interests include MAC protocols in wireless ad hoc networks, vehicular ad hoc networks, speech emotion recognition, multimodal learning, federated learning, and the Internet of Things.

From 2005 to 2008, I worked as a Senior Telecom Engineer and Team Leader at TMA Solutions in Vietnam. From 2008 to 2011, I served as Head of the Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering Department and Laboratory Head at [Ton Duc Thang University, Vietnam](https://tdtu.edu.vn/). From 2011 to 2014, I served as a Research Assistant and Postdoctoral Researcher at Kyung Hee University, Korea. From 2015 to 2017, I served as Head of the Electronicsâ€“Telecommunications Department at the Faculty of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Ton Duc Thang University. From 2017 to 2022, I served as Vice Dean of the School of Graduate Studies at the same university.

Currently, I am an Associate Professor at [FPT University](https://daihoc.fpt.edu.vn/en/), Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, and the Director of the [AiTA Lab](https://aita-lab.github.io/), where I lead research in artificial intelligence, telecommunications, and advanced technologies.

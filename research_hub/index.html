Tools and Resources for Academic Success

Create Academic Profiles

  • ORCID: Establish a unique researcher identifier to track your publications and ensure proper attribution across platforms. Visit ORCID
  • Scopus: Create a Scopus Author Profile to track your publications, citations, and h-index in this comprehensive database. Register for Scopus
  • Web of Science: Set up a Researcher Profile to monitor your publications and citation metrics across Web of Science databases. Create Web of Science Profile
  • ResearchGate: Build a professional network, share your research, and connect with peers in your field. Join ResearchGate
  • Google Scholar: Create a profile to showcase your publications, track citations, and monitor your research impact. Create Google Scholar Profile
  • Google Scholar Alerts: Set up customized alerts to receive notifications about new publications relevant to your research interests. Set Up Alerts
